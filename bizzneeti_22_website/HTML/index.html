About Bizzneeti

Bizzneeti is a top 10 National-level B-plan competition , hosted by the Students’ Council’s Business and Management Cell, Colloquium .With Bizzneeti, we aim to provide a platform to discover the untapped potential of young minds and turn their innovative ideas into reality.

The participants need to compete against each other in 2 comprehensive phases. These lucrative phases will test various aspects of their entrepreneurial idea – originality, research, monetary aspects, economic strategies, feasibility. In the concluding phase, the best teams get to pitch their ideas to highly accomplished Angel Investors.

Why should you participate in Bizzneeti


  • Bizzneeti provides you with an opportunity and platform to seek exposure, interact, and learn from a group of like-minded individuals.

  • You will be presented with an opportunity to pitch your ideas to a panel of highly esteemed Angel Investors.

  • If the judges are convinced by your business plan, and believe that it is feasible to sustain in the real world, you stand a chance to win from a cash pool of ₹ 1,00,000.

  • These include:
    • Winner: ₹ 85,000
    • First Runner-up: ₹ 25,000
    • Second runner-up: ₹ 15,000

Grant

In a fast paced world driven by changes in technology and innovation, businesses are constantly evolving. In recognition of that, Bizzneeti '22 is offering a special grant worth $10,000 - $25,000 for all those startups who are operating in the fields of Web3 & Crypto. This serves as an opportunity to develop their venture and explore new frontiers.
